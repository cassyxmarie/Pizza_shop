Pizza Shop

Established in 1995, Blind Onion is a neighborhood pizza joint offering great honest pizza, sandwiches, salads, and craft beers. We offer a casual nostalgic fun atmosphere. Located in NE Portland, our restaurant has a strong focus on delicious pizza, making it a popular hangout that fits perfectly in this community.
